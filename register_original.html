Our mission

As undergraduates, we witness a lot of waste on our campus. We realize that colleges everywhere are contributing large masses

of food waste to landfills, which is very damaging to the environment. We aim to reduce the amount of waste produced by colleges

by providing simple and local opportunities for colleges to make use of both their expired and non-expired ingredients.

By providing a networking system to connect colleges to local composting sites, gardens, and food banks, we hope to significantly

cut down the amount of food that gets delivered to landfills.
